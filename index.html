Ready to Go?

Working with me is simple and straightforward

1

Get in Touch

Let's discuss your project! To provide you with the best service, please share:

  • • Your script and intended usage (commercial advertising, corporate video, e-learning content, broadcast media, etc). Happy to provide a sample recording if you send the script.
  • • Your preferred tone and style (warm, authoritative, upbeat, conversational, etc) or a reference track you'd like me to match
  • • Your deadline and budget parameters
2

Review Quote & Sample

You'll receive a detailed quote covering all usage rights (commercial, broadcast, or standard licensing) along with a sample recording if requested. The quote includes clear timelines for delivery.

After you approve and we finalize the project details, I'll begin production. Any script revisions after this stage may require pricing adjustments to reflect the additional work.

3

Fast Turnaround & Delivery

Once approved, I'll record, edit, and master your voice-over. Most projects are completed and delivered within 24 hours of final approval.

Your finished files arrive with the invoice and licensing documentation. Choose your preferred format (WAV or MP3) and delivery method—email, WeTransfer, Dropbox, or Google Drive—whatever works best for your workflow.
