Ready to Go?

Working with me is simple and straightforward

1

Get in Touch

Let's discuss your project! To provide you with the best service, please share:

  • • Your script and intended usage (commercial, corporate, e-learning, broadcast, etc). Happy to provide a sample recording.
  • • Your preferred tone and style (warm, authoritative, upbeat, conversational, etc)
  • • Your deadline and budget parameters
2

Review Quote & Sample

You'll receive a detailed quote covering all usage rights along with a sample recording if requested. The quote includes clear timelines for delivery.

After you approve and we finalize the project details, I'll begin production.

3

Fast Turnaround & Delivery

Once approved, I'll record, edit, and master your voice-over. Most projects are delivered within 24 hours of final approval.

Your finished files arrive with invoice and licensing documentation in your preferred format (WAV or MP3).
